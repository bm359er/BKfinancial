Employee Retention

Safeguarding Your Workforce

In modern corporate operations, employee retention and tactical fiscal planning are tied to competitive advantage. By integrating rigorous data analytics and structuring customized risk-mitigation, we deliver employee benefits packages designed for small to medium-sized businesses across Canada.

Pension Funds & Retirement

Establishing highly competitive capital accumulation and corporate savings programs tailored to align with business-specific structures.

  • Group RRSPs and TFSAs: Tax-efficient contributions that support employee-centric wealth creation while minimizing corporate burden.
  • Deferred Profit-Sharing Plans (DPSPs): Incentivize workforce productivity by directly linking performance metrics to vested retirement capital.
  • Employee Retention: Employer contributions are only eligible to be paid to the employee until the employee has been with the company for a year

Group Insurance & Benefits

Constructing multi-tier protection, with no medical underwriting, aimed at sheltering your workforce from health interruptions.

  • Group Life Insurance: Essential foundational security providing solid economic indemnities to dependents and beneficiaries.
  • Critical Illness Coverage: Ensure employees has cash flow when dealing with complex diagnoses.
  • Comprehensive Extended Health: Fully tailored health, dental, and pharmaceutical packages designed to optimize structural operational wellness.
